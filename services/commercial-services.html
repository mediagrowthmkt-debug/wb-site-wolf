Commercial Services

Expert commercial construction and build-out solutions for offices, retail, and restaurants in Greater Boston.

Business Spaces

Spaces That Work
As Hard As You Do

Your business environment speaks volumes about your brand. At Wolf Carpenters, we specialize in building and transforming commercial spaces that are not only functional but also leave a lasting impression on your clients, employees, and visitors.

From the first consultation to the final walkthrough, our commercial construction team manages every detail — millwork, partitions, flooring, lighting, and fixtures — ensuring your project is delivered on schedule and within budget. We've completed commercial projects throughout Peabody, Salem, Lynn, Beverly, and Boston's North Shore.

We understand how critical it is to minimize downtime for your operations. Our team works efficiently and, where necessary, after hours or over weekends to keep your business running while we transform your space.

We serve a wide range of commercial clients across Greater Boston.

01

Office Build-Outs

Complete fit-outs for new office spaces including open-plan layouts, conference rooms, reception areas, private offices, and custom millwork built to your brand standards.

02

Retail Fit-Outs

Display cases, custom shelving, feature walls, fitting rooms, and full retail interior builds designed to maximize customer flow and reinforce your retail brand identity.

03

Restaurant & Hospitality

Bars, restaurants, cafes, and hospitality spaces built for durability and ambience. We build custom bars, booth seating, commercial kitchen cabinetry, and high-traffic flooring solutions.

04

Custom Millwork & Fixtures

Precision-built millwork including reception desks, storefront fixtures, paneling, wainscoting, and decorative elements that elevate your commercial interior above the competition.

05

Partitions & Feature Walls

Demising walls, glass partitions, accent panels, and architectural features that define zones within open commercial spaces while adding visual impact.

06

Minimal Disruption Scheduling

We offer after-hours and phased construction to keep your business operational during renovations. Your customers and staff experience minimal inconvenience throughout the project.
