Types of Additions

What We Build

From small room expansions to complete second stories. We've done it all in Peabody, MA & North Shore.

01

Room Additions

Expand your home with an extra bedroom, home office, family room, or sunroom. We match your existing architecture for a seamless result.

02

Second Story Addition

Double your square footage without expanding your footprint. We engineer, frame, and finish full second-story additions with minimal disruption.

03

Garage Addition

Attached or detached, we build single-car, double-car, and custom garage additions complete with proper drainage, insulation, and electrical.

Mudroom Addition

Create functional transition space with a custom mudroom addition featuring built-in storage, bench seating, cubbies, and durable flooring.

In-Law Suite

Build a private, self-contained living space for extended family, or generate rental income with a fully permitted accessory dwelling unit (ADU).

06

Bump-Out Addition

Extend an existing room by a few feet to gain meaningful additional space in your kitchen, bathroom, or master bedroom.
