Bathroom Renovations

Bathrooms That Feel
Like a Spa

A bathroom renovation is one of the highest-ROI investments you can make in your home — and no one delivers it better than Wolf Carpenters. We've remodeled bathrooms throughout Peabody, Salem, Lynn, Danvers, and Greater Boston, building everything from simple refreshes to complete gut renovations with luxury finishes.

Our bathroom remodeling services are fully comprehensive. We manage demo, waterproofing, cement board installation, tile work, shower builds, tub surrounds, vanity carpentry, fixture installation, lighting, and all the finish work. Every bathroom we build is waterproofed beyond code requirements so it stays beautiful for years.

Whether you're updating a powder room or redesigning a master bathroom suite, Wolf Carpenters brings the craftsmanship and attention to detail that separates a truly great bathroom from a generic renovation.

Bathroom Services

Bathroom Remodeling
Services We Offer

From quick refreshes to spa-worthy master bath renovations.

01

Full Gut Renovations

Complete bathroom demolition and rebuild — we strip everything to the studs, inspect for water damage, waterproof properly, and build your new bathroom from scratch with premium materials.

02

Tile & Shower Installation

Floor-to-ceiling tile work, shower pan builds, tub surrounds, and niche installation. We work with subway tile, large-format porcelain, natural stone, and mosaic accents.

03

Vanity & Fixture Installation

Custom vanity builds, freestanding or floating vanity installation, mirror framing, medicine cabinets, faucet and fixture installation, and toilet replacement with professional finish.

04

Waterproofing & Cement Board

We never skip the substrate. Every shower and wet area gets proper cement board, waterproof membrane, and moisture barrier installation before any tile goes up — the foundation of a lasting remodel.

05

Custom Built-Ins & Storage

Recessed shelving, built-in linen closets, shampoo niches, over-toilet cabinets, and bathroom vanity towers — designed and built to fit your space and maximize every square inch.

06

Heated Floor Systems

Electric radiant floor heating beneath your tile. Warms the floor in minutes using a programmable thermostat — a luxury upgrade that adds comfort and home value at a surprisingly affordable cost.
