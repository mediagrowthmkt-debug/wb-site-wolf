Kitchen Renovations

Kitchens Built for
How You Live

The kitchen is the most-used room in your home — and it deserves to be beautiful, functional, and built to last. Wolf Carpenters specializes in complete kitchen remodels that combine expert carpentry, precision installation, and thoughtful design to create spaces homeowners love for decades.

Our kitchen remodeling services cover everything: custom cabinetry built and installed to your exact specifications, countertop fabrication and installation, tile backsplash, appliance fit-outs, under-cabinet lighting, pantry built-ins, and full layout redesigns. We handle permits, demo, and coordination with your trades so you have a single point of contact throughout.

We serve homeowners throughout Peabody, Salem, Lynn, Beverly, Danvers, and Peabody, MA & North Shore. Whether you want a modern open-concept kitchen or a classic New England farmhouse look, we bring the craftsmanship to make it happen.

Kitchen Services

Everything Your Kitchen
Renovation Needs

End-to-end kitchen remodeling from demo to final cleanup.

01

Custom Cabinetry

Built-to-order kitchen cabinets in any style — shaker, flat-panel, inset, or raised-panel. Designed for your space, your storage needs, and your aesthetic vision.

02

Countertop Installation

Quartz, granite, marble, butcher block, or laminate — we install countertops with precision cuts, seamless seams, and waterproof edges for lasting beauty and function.

03

Backsplash & Tile

Subway tile, mosaic, herringbone, or stone slab backsplash installation behind the stove, counters, and throughout the kitchen with immaculate grout lines.

04

Full Layout Redesign

From galley to open-concept, we remove walls, relocate islands, add peninsulas, and reconfigure your kitchen's entire layout to maximize flow, light, and storage.

05

Appliance Fit-Out

Integrated appliance panels, range hood surround carpentry, dishwasher trim, and built-in refrigerator framing so every appliance looks like it belongs there.

06

Cabinet Refacing & Painting

Update the look of your existing kitchen without full replacement. We reface doors, replace drawer fronts, add new hardware, and paint or refinish cabinet boxes at a fraction of the cost.
