About Wolf Carpenters

Our Story

Building Greater Boston, One Project at a Time

Wolf Carpenters was founded with a single mission: to deliver superior construction and renovation services that stand the test of time. What started as a small carpentry operation has grown into a full-service construction company trusted by hundreds of homeowners and businesses across Greater Boston.

Led by Julio and his dedicated team, we bring decades of hands-on experience to every job — from a kitchen remodel to a complete commercial build-out. We're not just contractors; we're craftsmen who take pride in every nail we drive and every surface we finish.

Based in Peabody, MA, we serve all communities in the greater Boston area, including Salem, Lynn, Danvers, Beverly, and surrounding towns.
